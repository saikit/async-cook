# Pierogi and Carrot in Miso Butter

Cooking frozen pierogi in a dashi broth while steaming carrots in the microwave and combined with a miso butter sauce, this spin on a traditional Polish dish comes together in a flash.

## Ingredients

- **150g** carrots
<!---->
- **150g** mushrooms
<!---->
### For the dumplings

- **150g** frozen pierogi (: other stuffed pastas such as ravioli would also work)
- **150g** water or dashi
<!---->
- **15g** miso paste
- **15g** butter
<!---->
## Instructions

## Step 1 — Prepare carrots

Cut carrots to similar shape as the dumplings

## Step 2 — Prepare mushrooms *(optional)*

Chop mushrooms

## Step 3 — Cook dumplings

Cooking dumplings in water

## Step 4 — add miso butter

Add **miso** and **butter** to pasta
