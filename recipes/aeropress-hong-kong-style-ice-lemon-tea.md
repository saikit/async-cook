# Aeropress Hong Kong-style Ice Lemon Tea

Using the same techniques from the milk tea recipe to create another popular drink from Hong Kong

## Ingredients

### For the tea

- **3g** loose leaf black tea
- **200ml** water
<!---->
- **1** lemon
<!---->
- **100g** ice cubes
<!---->
- **5g** sugar
<!---->
<!---->
## Instructions

## Step 1 — Boil the tea

Add **black tea** and **boiling water** to a pot. Bring tea to a boil then simmer at low heat for **10 minutes**.

## Step 2 — Prepare lemon

Background: *Tea is simmering*

Cut a few slices if using. Zest the surface of the **lemon** if using. Juice the remainder. (🕑: Ideally, one lemon can produce at least 30ml of juice and 1-2g of zest. Both of these ingredients can be prepared in advance as frozen products.)

## Step 3 — Prepare lemons

Background: *Tea is simmering*

Add **lemon juice** and **ice cubes** to serving glass

## Step 4 — Extract the tea and blend

Setup Aeropress with a single paper filter. Add **sugar** and **lemon zest**, and pour **brewed tea**, including leaves, into the chamber. Stir to dissolve sugar.

Push the plunger through the chamber gently until all liquid is passed through.

## Step 5 — Serve lemon tea

Stir **lemon tea** and garnish with **lemon slices** if using
