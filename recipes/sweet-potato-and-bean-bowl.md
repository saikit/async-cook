# Sweet Potato and Bean bowl
## Ingredients

225g dried pinto beans
900g water
5g salt
1g baking soda

300-400g sweet potato

45g extra virgin olive oil
15g vinegar
1g black pepper

## Optional Step
Freeze sweet pototoes before cooking

## Step 1
Wash beans and then combine with salt, baking soda, water in an electric pressure cooker. Set pressure cooker to 50 minutes. Allow for natural release.

## Step 2
On a baking sheet covered with foil, place sweet potatoes in 450&deg;F oven for 50 minutes or until interior is soft.

## Step 3
Drain cooked beans. Combine with olive oil, vinegar, and black pepper. Peel cooked sweet potatoes and serve.
